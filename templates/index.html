COVID-19: Simulate Staffing Levels

COVID-19: Rota Test

Simulate what happens to staffing levels over time under different rates of staff infection.

About the Project

We have noticed that staff working on frontline services, such as healthcare workers, are becoming infected or self-isolating and this is placing great strain on the service they provide. To enable those in charge of rotas to see what happens under different scenarios we have created this web app. Like any model, it is based on some assumptions, however, where possible we have used available data (e.g. see references to the right).

If you would like to share information on staff infection rates in your area, please contact us and we will post the data. We will NOT share your name, location or institution, although we will share the working environment e.g. emergency unit, intensive care, delivery service etc.

We also provide a page for you to share the parameters of your simulation if you wish. Many of you will have innovative ways of working that others can benefit from.

We are continuing to develop our model. If you would like to help us in any way or provide feedback on the model or web app, please to contact us. We are trying to encourage debate in this area. If you can do better, please do so and tell us about it so we can post your efforts!

More Information

Please visit our documentation to see details about the employed model and web app or check the published rota data by other institudes and individuals. If you wish to start your own simulation, see below.

Run Your Own Simulation
