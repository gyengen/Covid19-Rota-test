Estimate the Total Number Of Staff over time

Our model is partially based on random variables. Therfore, the final results, as seen below, are generated by Monte Carlo method. The method is used to model the probability of different outcomes in a process that cannot easily be predicted due to the intervention of random variables.

{{output_div|safe}}

This figure represents the total available (blue line) and infected (green line) staff. The solid line represents the average of 100 simulations, the coloured area represents the 95% confidence interval.
